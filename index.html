Server Rules

1. Respect Everyone
No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination. Treat all players and staff with respect.
2. No Cheating or Exploits
Any use of hacks, exploits, or third-party software is strictly prohibited.
3. No Advertising
Do not advertise other servers or websites in chat or builds.
4. No Griefing
Do not destroy, steal, or sabotage other players’ builds or items.
5. No Spamming
Avoid excessive messages or repeated characters in chat.
6. Keep Builds Appropriate
All builds, skins, and usernames must be suitable for all ages.
7. Fair Trading
Do not scam, exploit, or manipulate other players during trades.
8. Protect Your Account
Do not share your login information with anyone.
9. Follow Staff Instructions
Respect all staff decisions to maintain a fair environment.
10. No AFK Farming Abuse
Do not exploit AFK mechanics or macroing to gain unfair advantages.
11. No Impersonation
Do not impersonate staff or other players in any way.
12. Use English in Public Chat
Keep global chat understandable for moderation and community.
13. No NSFW Content
Keep all builds, images, and messages safe for all players.
14. Don’t Exploit Bugs
Report bugs instead of using them to gain advantages.
15. Respect Server Economy
Do not manipulate or crash the in-game economy.
16. No Multi-Accounting
Each player may only have one account unless approved by staff.
17. Keep Chat Civil
No excessive arguing, flaming, or toxic behavior.
18. Don’t Ask for Staff
Do not repeatedly ask for admin or moderator status.
19. Report Issues Properly
Use the proper channels to report exploits, players, or bugs.
20. Have Fun!
Follow the rules, enjoy the server, and be part of a friendly community.
